# CreditTrust Financial Risk Dashboard

## Project Overview

CreditTrust is a financial institution that provides loans for various purposes, including business, education, personal, and vehicle financing. The organization faced increasing loan defaults and needed a data-driven approach to identify high-risk customers and improve lending decisions.

This project analyzes customer loan data to identify default risk patterns, segment customers based on financial risk, and provide actionable business recommendations. The analysis was conducted using Microsoft Power BI, leveraging data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), dashboard visualization, and customer segmentation techniques.

---

## Business Problem

Financial institutions face significant challenges in managing loan portfolios due to customer defaults. CreditTrust needed a solution to:

* Identify customers with a high probability of default.
* Understand factors contributing to repayment risk.
* Improve loan approval and risk assessment processes.
* Reduce financial losses from unpaid loans.
* Develop a customer risk segmentation framework.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer loan and financial data.
* Identify key factors associated with loan defaults.
* Create customer risk segments.
* Build an interactive dashboard for decision-making.
* Provide recommendations to improve lending performance.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* Power Query Editor
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Visualization Techniques

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query Editor.

### Cleaning Activities

* Checked for missing values.
* Removed duplicate customer records.
* Corrected data types.
* Standardized text values.
* Validated formatting consistency.
* Created calculated columns for advanced analysis.

### Calculated Fields

* Credit Score Band
* Income Band
* Age Group
* Risk Score
* Risk Tier

These transformations improved data quality and ensured reliable analysis.

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The analysis revealed several important customer behavior patterns:

* Customers with lower credit scores had significantly higher default rates.
* Unemployed customers recorded the highest loan default rates.
* Previous default history strongly increased the likelihood of future defaults.
* Lower-income customers demonstrated higher repayment risk.
* Car loans and education loans showed relatively higher default tendencies.

## Risk Segmentation

Customers were categorized into:

### High Risk

Customers with:

* Low credit scores
* Previous default history
* Unstable employment
* Low income levels

### Medium Risk

Customers with moderate financial profiles and manageable repayment risk.

### Low Risk

Customers with:

* Strong credit history
* Stable employment
* Higher income levels
* Low probability of default

---

## Key Insights

* Medium-risk customers formed the largest customer segment.
* High-risk customers contributed disproportionately to loan defaults.
* Previous default history was the strongest predictor of future default.
* Lower-income customers exhibited higher repayment challenges.
* Stable employment significantly improved repayment behavior.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Implement stricter approval criteria for customers with previous default history.
2. Introduce minimum credit score thresholds for loan approval.
3. Reduce loan exposure to high-risk borrowers.
4. Strengthen income verification processes for large loan applications.
5. Improve ongoing customer risk monitoring systems.
6. Incorporate risk segmentation into lending decisions.

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to strengthen financial risk management and improve lending decisions. By leveraging Power BI for data preparation, visualization, and customer segmentation, valuable insights were uncovered that can help CreditTrust reduce loan defaults, improve portfolio performance, and enhance overall business profitability.
